Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) are community groups for college and university students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.here at mvjce we carry events,drills and competitions to help fellow community to grow versatile in upcoming technologies and also we help them to upskill themself.
